The Dragon-Elf Bonds

Before the war, dragons and elves shared a unique relationship:

  • Telepathic bonds formed voluntarily
  • Dragons chose their riders
  • 48 Dragon Knights served the Empire
  • The bonds represented trust between species
  • They'd existed for centuries

Why Thaelen Survived

Thaelen and Thorongil were on a mission when the war started. By the time they returned, the worst was over. Their bond held because they never had to fight each other's kin.

Thorongil chose his rider over his species. He's considered a traitor by dragonkind.

The Hidden Truth

The official history is a lie. The real story:

  1. The war began over territory, not dragon torture
  2. Elves struck first against drow settlements encroaching on dragon lands
  3. Both sides committed atrocities
  4. The dragon torture was realâ€”but it was retaliation, not the cause
  5. The genocide was Valandor's rage, not justice

Who Knows

  • Emperor Valandor: Knows everything. Made the decision to bury it.
  • Velindor Shadowmere: Master of Whispers. Has killed to keep this secret.
  • The Living Archive: Contains sealed records. Thaelorin guards them.
  • Thaelen Ruincalen: Discovered the truth. It's why he defected.

What the Drow Did

After elven attacks on their settlements, drow scholars developed a theory: if they could force dragon bonds like elves had, they could defend themselves.

They captured three young dragons. They tortured them trying to create artificial bonds. It didn't work. The dragons died. But not before one escaped and told the others what had been done.

The dragons didn't distinguish between elf and drow. To them, the subterranean cousins looked identical. They attacked everyone.

Why Valandor Lied

The truth would destroy the Empire's moral foundation:

  • Elves started the conflict (territorial aggression)
  • Drow responded to elven attacks (not unprovoked evil)
  • 400 years of genocide were based on a lie
  • Valandor knew and ordered it anyway

If this became public, the Empire would tear itself apart.

Thaelen's Discovery

80 years ago, Thaelen found evidence in the Living Archive. He confronted Velindor. Velindor confirmed it.

Thaelen was ordered to forget what he learned. Instead, he defectedâ€”taking the last dragon with him.

His choice: Stay and serve an empire built on genocide and lies, or leave with Vera and build something honest.
