Exile from Dragonkind

Thorongil chose his bond with Thaelen over his species. He's been exiled from dragonkind for 400 years.

  • Cannot return to dragon society
  • Dragons in the northwestern mountains (400 years, no contact)
  • They consider him ultimate traitor
  • If he returned, they'd likely attack
  • No one to speak Draconic with
  • Sometimes flies toward the mountains, stops before visible

His Love for Vera

Thorongil loves Vera as much as Thaelen. This is unusualâ€”dragons bond with one rider, tolerating others at best. Thorongil genuinely chose to protect both of them.

Why? He saw something in Vera that reminded him of dragons. Her rage when she split the mountain. Her willingness to burn everything for the one she loved. He recognized a kindred spirit.

If Vera died: Thorongil might not leave. He might stay to protect what she built. Or he might burn both empires in grief. Even Thaelen doesn't know which.

His Role as Vera's Conscience

Vera visits 3-4 times weekly for "evening conversations":

  • Challenges her reasoning on difficult decisions
  • Points out blind spots she can't see
  • Validates her doubts (not weakness, wisdom)
  • Tells her when she's wrong (dragons don't lie)
  • He's the only one she told about the halfling girl

His Fears

  • Losing Thaelen: The bond might literally kill him. Thaelen has maybe 200-300 years left. Then what?
  • Losing Vera: Would devastate him independently now
  • The After: If both die, what does he do? Stay? Return to face death? Fly away alone?
  • Meaninglessness: Dragons don't fear death. They fear their existence having no purpose.
