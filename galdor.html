üèπ Galdor Ruincalen

The Last Dragon Rider - 400 Years of War and Wisdom

Race: High Elf
Age: 400+ years old
Class: Fighter/Ranger (Dragon Rider specialization)
Title: Co-Founder and Co-Ruler of Veraheim, "The Last Dragon Rider," "The Dragon Knight"
Status: Only living dragon rider with intact bond in all of Exilium

Core Character: Stoic Professional with Deep Regrets

In Public: Calm, controlled, professional. Emotions carefully managed. Leads by example, not speeches.

In Private: Warmer, but still measured. Carries weight of 400 years. Allows himself to feel in safe spaces (Vera, Thorongil).

Key Traits:

  • Stoic: Doesn't display emotion easily
  • Dutiful: Responsibility before personal feelings
  • Competent: 400 years of experience shows in everything
  • Regretful: Haunted by Dragon War and his narrow-minded past
  • Loyal: Absolute devotion to those he loves
  • Pragmatic: Focuses on what can be controlled

Combat Abilities

The Famous Dragon-Rider Archer

What Everyone Knows: Legendary archer from dragonback. Can hit targets at impossible distances. Aerial combat specialist. Fought in the Dragon War.

Famous Tactics:

  • Thorongil flies, Galdor shoots from his back
  • Precision strikes while moving at high speed
  • Can target specific weak points (joints, eyes, gaps)
  • Coordinated attacks via telepathic bond
  • Devastating against ground forces (no cover from above)

The Secret: Deadly with a Greatsword

What Most People Don't Know: Equally deadly in melee combat. Wields greatsword with perfect form. 400 years of blade training. Close-quarters dragon-fighting experience.

Why It's Secret: Archery is famous, overshadows everything else. Element of surprise in combat. Enemies plan for archer, get swordmaster.

Role in Veraheim

Supreme Commander of the Guard (300 Guards)

Command Structure:

  • Galdor: Supreme Commander (overall strategy, final authority)
  • 3 Shift Commanders: Report directly to Galdor
  • 12 Lieutenants: Lead specific units
  • 285 Guards: Four rotating shifts of 75 each

His Leadership Style:

  • Hands-on: Trains with guards regularly (3x per week minimum)
  • Morning drills, personal evaluation of every recruit
  • Guards respect him because he's one of them
  • "Never ask someone to do what you won't do yourself"

Project Implementation Overseer

Follows all major city projects under implementation. Ensures practical execution of Vera's plans. "Will this actually work?" perspective. 400 years = seen every project type.

Daily Routine

Pre-Dawn:

  • Wakes before sunrise (military habit)
  • Checks overnight reports from night shift
  • Brief meeting with night shift commander

Dawn:

  • Private breakfast with Vera (their sacred time)
  • Discusses city business, personal matters
  • Only time he fully relaxes

Morning:

  • Guard training (3x per week) - drills, weapons practice, tactical scenarios
  • OR Strategic planning (alternate days) - defense reviews, intelligence analysis

Afternoon:

  • Project site inspections - visits 2-3 ongoing projects daily
  • Talks with workers, identifies problems
  • Administrative work - schedules, supplies, personnel

Evening:

  • Private dinner with Vera
  • Time with Thorongil (2-3x per week) - silent companionship, bond doesn't need words

The Regrets He Carries

1. The Dragon War (400 Years Ago)

What Haunts Him: Fought dragons for 35 days. Saw most dragon-elf bonds break or end in death. Beat dragons into retreat without killing them. Survived when others didn't.

The Guilt: Why him? Why his dragon? Survivor's guilt for 400 years. 320 years as last dragon rider before meeting Vera.

2. The Mission to Kill Vera

The Orders: Elven Empire sent him to eliminate Vera. He went intending to kill her. Didn't question the orders.

What He Regrets: His automatic obedience, his prejudice, his narrow-mindedness. Almost killed the love of his life. "I was prepared to murder an innocent. What does that make me?"

3. What He Lost vs What He Gained

Lost: His people, his position, his culture, his homeland
Gained: Vera, purpose, freedom, home, peace
The Balance: Worth it? Yes. But he still mourns what he lost.

Relationship with Vera

80 Years of Partnership - Still Deeply in Love

  • Still passionate after 80 years (not just comfortable)
  • The fire has matured but hasn't dimmed
  • Run the city together as true partners
  • Public: Professional, united front
  • Private: Warm, affectionate, playful
  • Arguments are productive (she: urgency, he: patience)
  • Never go to bed angry (their 80-year rule)

What He Provides:

  • Military expertise and security
  • Patience (elven perspective balances human urgency)
  • Support (questions privately, supports publicly)
  • Stability (400 years = steady hand)
  • Grounding (reminds her to rest)

Relationship with Thorongil

400+ Years of Unbreakable Bond

The Sacred Connection: Bonded for 400+ years. Survived Dragon War together. Shared exile from their peoples. Understand each other without words.

How They Communicate:

  • Telepathic bond (mind-to-mind)
  • Emotions shared directly
  • Don't need to speak (but sometimes do)
  • 400 years = perfect understanding

What Thorongil Means to Galdor:

  • Brother (closer than family)
  • Partner (in every battle)
  • Witness (to his entire adult life)
  • Anchor (when everything else changed, Thorongil remained)
  • For 320 years, Thorongil was his only companion

Their Routine:

  • Evening visits (2-3 times per week)
  • Sometimes conversation, often just silent companionship
  • They watch the sunset together
  • "Being with him is like coming home"

"Traitor who stole our last dragon and sided with a human." C√≠rdan views him as the one that got away. Ecthelion personally feels the loss.
