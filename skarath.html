The Iron-Wood Pact

The most important economic relationship in the Broken Isles:

  • Skarath provides: Timber, charcoal, rope, ships
  • Ironhold provides: Anchors, chains, fittings, weapons
  • Fixed prices: 15% below market for both parties
  • Priority fulfillment: Each other's orders come first
"They forge the sinew. We build the bones. Neither works without the other."
