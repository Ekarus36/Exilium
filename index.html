The World

Exilium is a D&D 5e campaign setting where two ancient powers dominate: the Elven Empire in the west and the Human Kingdom in the east. Both nations enforce strict racial hierarchies, persecuting those who don't fit their vision of purity.

Between them, hidden in mountains accessible only through an underground river, lies Veraheimâ€”a sanctuary city founded 80 years ago by three unlikely allies: a human archmage, a high elf ranger, and an ancient silver dragon. Here, the persecuted find refuge... but resources are finite, and idealism has limits.

Two days' sail east of Veraheim, the Broken Isles offer a different kind of freedomâ€”a pirate confederation where outcasts, exiles, and raiders forge their own destiny beyond the reach of kings and emperors.
