The World

Exilium is a D&D 5e campaign setting where two ancient powers dominate: the Elven Empire and the Human Kingdom. Both are deeply racist, favoring their own kind above all others.

80 years ago, everything changed when a powerful human sorcerer and an elven Dragon Knight fell in love, creating Veraheimâ€”a city that welcomes the unwanted, the exiled, the refugees of Exilium.
