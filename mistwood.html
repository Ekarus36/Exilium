The Old Places

Mistwood contains ancient ruins of unknown origin‚Äîstone structures overgrown by forest, underground complexes in cave systems. No one knows who built them.

  • Predates elves, humans, all known races
  • Strange materials that don't corrode
  • Writing no one can fully read
  • Traps still functional after ages
  • Seeker Bands carefully extract valuable artifacts
"The Old Places hold secrets. We know how to ask."

Monster Hunting

"Others run from monsters. We hunt them."

Mistwood hosts dangerous creatures. The Shadowpaw specialize in killing them and selling the parts.

  • Mist Stalkers, Cavern Crawlers, Forest Trolls
  • Giant Spiders, Bog Wyrms
  • Parts sold to alchemists, crafters, collectors
  • Bones sold to Stormcrag orcs

Trader's Edge

The only permanent structure‚Äîa trading post on the southern coast where outsiders come to trade. No outsider goes beyond Trader's Edge.
