What anyone in Exilium would know

Magic exists in Exilium, but how it's treated depends entirely on where you are and what type you practice.

Sorcerers (born with magic) are the most controversialâ€”celebrated in the Empire, hunted in the Kingdom, welcomed in Veraheim.

Wizards (learned magic) are accepted everywhere but restricted by class in the Kingdom.

Divine casters (clerics, paladins) are universally acceptedâ€”they serve the gods.

Warlocks don't exist yet. The Great Storm blocks extraplanar contact.

ğŸ” Detection

The Problem

Detecting sorcerous ability before manifestation is nearly impossible. A child might be 15 before their power emerges. By then, they've lived their whole life in a community.

Thorongil's Gift

The dragon Thorongil can sense sorcerous manifestation within approximately 5 miles. He's detected several young sorcerers in Veraheim's territory and quietly arranged their protection.

This is unique. No other reliable detection method exists. The Kingdom relies on informants and observation of unusual events.

Signs of Manifestation

How sorcerous power typically reveals itself:

  • Emotional outbursts causing magical effects
  • Objects moving or changing without explanation
  • Strange dreams that affect reality
  • Unusual resistance to harm
  • Accidental fire, ice, or lightning during stress

The Great Storm's Nature

The Great Storm isn't natural. Something created it. The oldest dragons remember a time before it existedâ€”when extraplanar contact was possible, when entities from beyond could walk the world.

What ended that era? No one knows. The Storm appeared, and the boundaries sealed. Some scholars believe it was divine intervention. Others think it was a magical catastrophe. A few suggest it was deliberateâ€”protection against something worse.

The Storm Is Weakening

In the past few decades, isolated incidents suggest the Great Storm may be failing:

  • Dreams featuring entities that shouldn't exist
  • Whispers at the edges of magic
  • A few individuals showing powers that don't fit any category

If the Storm fails entirely: Extraplanar entities could enter the world. Warlocks could form. Ancient evils might return. The entire magical order could collapse.

Vera's True Power

Vera Wyrdweaver is the most powerful sorcerer aliveâ€”but even she doesn't fully understand her limits. Her magic has no clear ceiling. She's split mountains. She could potentially do more.

What she fears: That she might be proof the Storm is weakening. That her power comes from somewhere else. That something is watching through her.
