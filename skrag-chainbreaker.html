Legendary Captain

Skrag Chainbreaker

"The Slaver"

Overview

Skrag Chainbreaker is the Broken Isles' primary slave trader‚Äîa bugbear captain who runs a fleet of 8 ships dedicated to capturing, transporting, and selling living cargo. His name is ironic: he breaks chains only to put new ones on. He's the captain other pirates avoid at taverns, even those with no moral objections to his trade.

Race Bugbear
Age 42
Title The Slaver
Fleet 8 ships
Flagship Mercy's End
Clan Origin Shadowpaw

Appearance

  • Build: Large even for a bugbear, heavy with muscle turning slightly to fat
  • Fur: Dark brown, well-groomed (he's vain about appearance)
  • Eyes: Yellow, constantly calculating value
  • Clothing: Expensive‚Äîsilks and fine leather, gold jewelry
  • Weapons: Heavy flail, whip at belt (for "merchandise management")

Personality

The Businessman

  • Views slavery as purely business‚Äîno different than any other cargo
  • Keeps meticulous records of inventory and sales
  • Provides "quality product" to maintain reputation
  • Not cruel for cruelty's sake‚Äîdamaged goods sell for less

The Pragmatist

  • No moral framework about slavery‚Äîit exists, he profits
  • Will sell anyone: human, elf, orc, doesn't matter
  • Won't enslave fellow Isles residents (bad for business)
  • Professional about the whole thing, which makes it worse

The Trade

Acquisition

  • Raids coastal villages for "fresh stock"
  • Purchases from other pirates who capture but don't want to deal
  • Buys prisoners from mainland conflicts
  • Accepts "debt payments" from desperate people

Processing

  • Maintains holding facilities on Mistwood (his clan's territory)
  • "Breaks" difficult merchandise
  • Sorts by value: labor, domestic, skilled, "specialty"
  • Basic medical care (protects investment)

Sales

  • Primary market: Saltmere's Flesh Market
  • Buyers from across the known world
  • Private sales for high-value merchandise
  • Standing contracts with regular customers

Relationship with Shadowpaw Clan

Skrag is technically Shadowpaw‚Äîborn to the Mistwood bugbear clan. But his relationship with his people is... complicated.

  • He's clan, so he has certain rights
  • Many Shadowpaw disapprove of slaving
  • His money is useful to the clan
  • His reputation reflects poorly on them
  • Chief Varka tolerates him, doesn't embrace him
"Skrag is Shadowpaw. We don't choose our family."

Reputation

Among Other Pirates

  • Useful for handling prisoners they don't want
  • Avoided socially‚Äîthe trade makes people uncomfortable
  • Respected for business acumen, not liked
  • "Even pirates have limits. Skrag doesn't."

Among Victims

Coastal communities whisper his name as a warning. Ships fly no flag rather than risk being identified when his sails appear.

Why He's Tolerated

  • Slavery is legal in the Broken Isles
  • He pays tribute, follows Harbor Peace
  • Provides service other pirates use
  • Doesn't enslave Isles residents
  • Money flows to the confederation

The Broken Isles isn't Veraheim. Morality is flexible when profit is involved.
