Geography: The Caldera Harbor

The harbor is a geological marvel‚Äîan ancient volcanic crater that collapsed and flooded, creating a nearly perfect natural port.

  • Single narrow entrance ‚Äî easily defended, ships must enter single-file
  • Deep water ‚Äî can accommodate hundreds of ships
  • Volcanic cliffs ‚Äî surround the harbor on all sides
  • Protected from storms ‚Äî the caldera walls block most weather
  • Natural hot springs ‚Äî volcanic activity provides heated pools

The Throat

The narrow channel into the harbor is called "The Throat" by sailors:

  • Only one ship can pass at a time
  • Defended by shore batteries on both cliffs
  • Any attacking fleet would be destroyed ship by ship
  • This geography makes Freehold nearly impregnable

The Sacred Law: Harbor Peace

"Any ship heading to or from Freehold is untouchable. Break this rule and die."

The Rule

  • Ships traveling TO Freehold = protected
  • Ships traveling FROM Freehold = protected
  • Within Freehold: No violence against other pirates
  • No theft from other pirates
  • All disputes settled through proper channels

Enforcement

Breaking Harbor Peace = Death

  • Not from the Monarch alone‚Äîfrom ALL captains collectively
  • Your ship burned, your crew scattered
  • Your name erased from the Isles
  • No one has broken the Peace in decades
  • The Storm would act if Peace were broken

The Moot

The governing council of the Broken Isles meets quarterly in the Gathering Hall near the Monarch's Keep.

Composition

  • Each pirate captain = 1 vote
  • Each settlement = 1 vote
  • Pirate Monarch = tie-breaker

Responsibilities

  • Elect/confirm the Pirate Monarch
  • Settle disputes between captains
  • Coordinate major raids
  • Handle external threats

  • The Shipbreaker's Yard: Where captured ships are dismantled
