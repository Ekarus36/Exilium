Harbor Peace

The sacred law that makes everything work:

  • No violence within Freehold's waters
  • Ships traveling to/from Freehold are protected
  • Violators: Hunted by ALL captains, executed publicly
  • Disputes: Settled by the Queen or dueling (outside harbor)

Why it works: Without Harbor Peace, no one could safely trade. Merchants couldn't fence goods. Captains couldn't resupply. Everyone benefits. Everyone enforces.

The Moot

Quarterly assembly at the Throne Hall:

  • Every captain = 1 vote
  • Every settlement = 1 vote
  • Pirate Monarch = tie-breaker
  • Major decisions require 2/3 majority

Economy

The Caldera's Secret

The volcanic caldera wasn't entirely natural. Something carved the entrance channel. Something reinforced the walls. Ancient construction, far older than the pirate confederation.

What lies beneath: Flooded tunnels below the harbor floor lead to chambers carved into the rock. Only the oldest captains know. They don't talk about it. Some things are best left underwater.
