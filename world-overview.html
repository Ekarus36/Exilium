The Core Premise

Exilium is a world dominated by two racist superpowers separated by mountains. The Elven Empire to the west believes in elven supremacy. The Human Kingdom to the east believes in human supremacy. Both treat other races as second-class citizens at best.

80 years ago, a human sorceress and an elven dragon rider fell in love, defied both nations, and created Veraheimâ€”a sanctuary city carved into the mountains where all races are equal. A dragon guards it. The only safe passage between east and west runs beneath it.

The central question: What is the price of idealism? How much can you save when resources are limited? When does pragmatism become cruelty?
