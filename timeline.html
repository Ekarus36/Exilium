The Elves-Dragon War (400 Years Ago)

The Crime

  • Drow capture and torture dragons in hidden cities
  • Attempt to force dragon bonds through dark magic
  • Experimentation on captured dragons
  • Done in secret, far from elven observation

The Mistake

  • Elder dragons discover the experiments
  • Assume ELVES are responsible (riders are elves)
  • Launch surprise attack on Elven Empire

The War (35 Days)

  • Devastating despite brevity
  • Bonded dragons face impossible choice
  • Most bonds broken or ended in death
  • Galdor & Thorongil: sole survivors with intact bond
  • Galdor never kills a dragon, only defeats them
  • His dragon considered traitor by other dragons

The Aftermath

  • All dragon-elf bonds lost except Galdor's
  • Dragons retreat to northwestern mountains
  • No contact between species for 400 years
  • Dragon Knights become warriors without dragons

The Discovery (Years Later)

  • Pengolodh and Council of Memory investigate
  • Truth revealed: DROW caused everything

  • Drow Persecution: Kill on sight, 400 years and counting

The Founding of Veraheim (80 Years Ago)

The Crisis

  • Mountain pass between Empire and Kingdom collapsing
  • Lives at risk on both sides
  • Trade route threatened

The Meeting

  • Galdor sent by Empire to eliminate Vera Wyrdweaver
  • Vera was powerful human sorcerer, viewed as threat
  • They met during the catastrophe
  • Chose to save lives together instead of fighting

The Love

  • Fell in love after saving countless lives
  • Both powers outraged at their union
  • Human sorcerer + elven dragon rider = forbidden

The Hunt

  • Elven Empire: Accused Vera of enchanting Galdor
  • Human Kingdom: Accused elves of kidnapping Vera
  • Both sent forces to capture them
  • Needless violence and bloodshed

The Split

  • Vera's anger manifested her full power
  • Split the mountain itself
  • Created sanctuary: Veraheim ("Vera's Home")
  • Safe haven for the unwanted and exiled
  • Carved tunnel through mountain for trade

The Loss

  • Empire lost last dragon rider
  • Kingdom lost most powerful sorcerer
  • Both powers now depend on Veraheim's tunnel
  • Uneasy status quo established
