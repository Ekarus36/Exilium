Overview

Rennick Vance is unique among the legendary captains‚Äîhe commands only a single ship, The Last Dance. His legendary status comes not from fleet size but from personal combat prowess. He is considered the deadliest duelist in the Broken Isles, possibly the entire known world, and has killed more captains in single combat than most pirates kill in their entire careers.

The Ship: The Last Dance

A fast, sleek vessel built by Skarath's finest Sketchmaster (Tizzi Three-Fingers). Designed for speed over firepower‚ÄîRennick doesn't need cannons when he can board and kill the enemy captain personally.

  • Crew of ~40 (small but elite)
  • Fastest ship in the Isles (except possibly Sorrow)
  • Light armament‚Äîrelies on speed and boarding

Why He's Legendary

Fleet size determines most captains' power. Rennick breaks this rule. One ship, forty crew‚Äîbut everyone knows that challenging him means facing him in single combat. And facing Rennick in single combat means dying.

The Captain's Gambit

His standard tactic: approach a target, signal for the enemy captain to meet him in single combat. If the captain wins, Rennick's crew surrenders. If Rennick wins, the enemy ship surrenders.

No captain has ever won.
