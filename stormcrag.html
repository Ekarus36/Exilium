Orc Clan Territories

Stormcrag

"The land feeds us. The sea provides. The bones remember. This is the orc way."

Overview

Stormcrag is the second-largest island in The Broken Isles, dominated by two orc clans who have transformed it into the agricultural and bonecraft heart of the confederation. Stormcrag feeds approximately 60% of the Broken Isles.

Total Population ~5,500
Bloodstone Clan ~3,500
Ironfang Clan ~2,000
Alliance Leader Warchief Gorrath
Clan Fleet 12 ships
Primary Export Food, Bonecraft

The Alliance

Twenty years ago, Gorrath Bloodstone forged peace between the traditionally rival clans through marriage alliance and shared purpose. The Clan Fleet unites their naval power.

Bonecraft

"Metal rusts. Wood rots. Bone endures. Bone remembers."

The orcs of Stormcrag have perfected the art of working with bones, shells, horns, and other natural materials.

The Meaning of Bone

"When a creature dies, its spirit lingers in the bones. We shape the bone, we honor the spirit. Our weapons carry the strength of the fallen."

Important bone items are named: "Storm's Tooth" (whale-bone harpoon), "Grandmother's Shield" (reinforced with ancestor's bones).
