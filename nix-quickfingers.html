The Swarm Tactics

Nix's fleet operates unlike any other in the Isles:

The Ships

  • 30+ small vessels (skiffs, cutters, converted fishing boats)
  • Each carries 10-20 goblins
  • Fast, maneuverable, expendable
  • Built or modified at Skarath (family discount)

The Method

  1. Swarm surrounds target
  2. Multiple boarding attempts simultaneously
  3. Overwhelm through numbers
  4. If target flees, the swarm follows
  5. If target fights, goblins pour aboard from every direction
