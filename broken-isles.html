The Broken Isles

"Freedom through strength. No kings, no chains, no laws but what we make."

Overview

The Broken Isles is a pirate confederation located two days' sail east of Veraheim's coast. An archipelago of approximately 20 islands (5 major, 15 smaller) of volcanic origin, it serves as home to the outcasts, exiles, and raiders who don't fit in the Empire, Kingdom, or even Veraheim.

Unlike the rigid hierarchies of the mainland powers, the Broken Isles operates through a loose confederation of independent settlements and pirate captains united by mutual benefit and the threat of external enemies.

Government Structure

The Moot

The governing council of the Broken Isles, meeting quarterly at Freehold Harbor.

  • Each pirate captain = 1 vote
  • Each settlement = 1 vote
  • Pirate Monarch = tie-breaker
  • Major decisions require 2/3 majority

The Pirate Monarch

Current: Graza Bloodtide (Queen, 3 years)

  • Commands the largest official fleet (25 ships)
  • Collects tribute from all captains
  • Enforces Harbor Peace
  • Settles major disputes
  • Must be confirmed by The Moot

The Sacred Law: Harbor Peace

"Any ship heading to or from Freehold is untouchable. Break this rule and die."
  • Ships traveling TO or FROM Freehold = protected
  • Protection lasts until they leave designated sea lanes
  • Violation = death sentence from ALL captains
  • Within Freehold: No violence in the harbor
  • Enforced by collective action (and ultimately by The Storm)
