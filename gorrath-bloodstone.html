Legendary Captain

Gorrath Bloodstone

"Blood and bone. Clan and sea. This is what we are."

Overview

Gorrath Bloodstone is the Warchief of the Bloodstone Clan and commander of the Orc Clan Fleet‚Äî12 warships crewed by warriors from both Bloodstone and allied Ironfang clans. Twenty years ago, he forged an unprecedented alliance between the traditionally rival orc clans of Stormcrag, creating the first unified orc naval power in the Broken Isles.

Appearance

  • Build: Massive even by orc standards, heavily muscled from decades of combat
  • Skin: Dark green, weathered by sun and salt
  • Scars: Numerous battle scars, including a prominent one across his left eye (still functional)
  • Tusks: Large, one capped with silver (replaced after combat damage)
  • Armor: Wears a mix of metal plate and traditional orc bonecraft‚Äîa statement of dual identity

Personality

The Warchief

  • Commands through respect earned in battle, not fear
  • Values honor, but defines it practically‚Äîkeeping your word, protecting your people
  • Patient strategist despite the "savage orc" stereotypes
  • Genuinely cares about his clans' welfare and future

The Politician

  • Forged alliance between ancient rivals (Bloodstone and Ironfang)
  • Maintains relationships with other captains and settlements
  • Understands that trade and diplomacy serve orc interests as much as raiding

The Clan Alliance

Twenty years ago, Gorrath accomplished what generations of Warchiefs had failed to do‚Äîhe united the Bloodstone and Ironfang clans. Not through conquest, but through marriage alliance and shared purpose.

How He Did It

  • His sister married Chief Mulga Ironfang's eldest son
  • Proposed the Clan Fleet as joint venture‚Äîshared glory, shared profit
  • Demonstrated that fighting each other only weakened both against outsiders
  • Patient diplomacy over several years

Current Status

  • Alliance holds strong after 20 years
  • Bloodstone (~3,500) provides most warriors
  • Ironfang (~2,000) provides farming expertise and steady supplies
  • Both clans benefit from Clan Fleet profits

Relationships

With Queen Graza

Respects her as a fellow half-blood who earned power. Loyal supporter at The Moot. Both understand the value of stable confederation.

With The Storm

Deep respect. The Storm's role in founding the confederation saved the orcs as much as anyone. Gorrath never forgets that debt.

With Skarath

Essential trade partner. Clan Fleet ships built by goblins. Stormcrag supplies food to Skarath. Mutual dependence creates good relations.

Goals

  • Maintain clan alliance for next generation
  • Expand Clan Fleet influence in confederation politics
  • Secure Stormcrag's prosperity through trade and raiding
  • Eventually: establish orc-controlled trade routes?
