Military Structure

Ironhold doesn't separate military from civilian‚Äîeveryone serves.

  • All hobgoblins serve from age 14
  • First 4 years: military training
  • After 4 years: assigned to division based on aptitude
  • Can be recalled to military duty at any time
  • "Every smith is a soldier. Every soldier can smith."

Standing Forces

  • 2,000 trained soldiers (can double by calling workers)
