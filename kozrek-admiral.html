Legendary Captain

Kozrek the Admiral

"Discipline. Formation. Victory. This is how proper navies fight."

Overview

Kozrek is a hobgoblin captain who styles himself "The Admiral" and runs his fleet like a military navy rather than a pirate band. Twelve years ago, he captured an actual Empire admiral and took the title along with valuable naval knowledge. His 15-20 ship fleet operates with military precision that other pirates find both impressive and insufferable.

Origin of "The Admiral"

Twelve years ago, Kozrek captured an Empire warship whose admiral hadn't gone down with the ship. Instead of ransoming or killing him, Kozrek spent three months extracting every piece of naval knowledge the man possessed‚Äîtactics, formations, supply chain management, crew discipline methods.

Then he took the title for himself. "If I know what he knew, I am what he was."
