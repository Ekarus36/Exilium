Saltmere

"Everything has a price. Everything is for sale. Welcome to Saltmere."

Overview

Saltmere is the commercial heart of The Broken Isles‚Äîa bustling free port where the products of every settlement flow together and meet buyers from across the world. This is where goblin ships are sold, hobgoblin weapons traded, orc bonecraft displayed, bugbear relics auctioned, and pirate plunder fenced.

If Freehold Harbor is the political capital, Saltmere is the economic one.

The Saltcouncil

Saltmere is ruled by merchants, for merchants.

  • First Merchant: Delara Silvertongue (half-elf, 67, rules 19 years)
  • Six Merchant Councilors: Seats effectively purchased
  • Philosophy: "Gold speaks. We listen."

The Counting Houses

The financial district where real power resides:

  • House Silvertongue: Largest, most influential
  • House Coinward: Ship financing specialists (goblin-run)
  • House Redgold: Weapons trade financing (hobgoblin-run)
  • House Wavecrest: Insurance specialists

Services

The Fence System

How stolen goods become legitimate:

  1. Pirate brings cargo to registered Fence merchant
  2. Fence assesses value, pays pirate (minus 15-50% fee)
  3. Goods entered into Fence registry (new ownership)
  4. Sold to buyers as "salvage" or "recovered cargo"
  5. Buyers get paperwork, plausible deniability
