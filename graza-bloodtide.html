The Storm's Shadow

Graza knows the truth: she rules because Vaelith lets her. If she ever truly challenged him, she'd lose.

Her calculation:

  • The Storm doesn't want to rule (too much politics)
  • He needs someone to handle daily governance
  • She's competent and doesn't threaten him
  • As long as she doesn't cross him, she's useful

What haunts her: Is she really a queen, or just his puppet? Does anyone respect her, or just fear The Storm through her?

Her Long Game

Graza isn't content to be a figurehead forever. Her strategy:

  1. Build legitimacy: Make decisions that benefit everyone (done)
  2. Strengthen fleet: Commission ships from Skarath (in progress)
  3. Cultivate allies: Captains loyal to HER, not tradition (ongoing)
  4. Wait: The Storm is mortal. Eventually he'll die. (Theoretically.)

The fear: What if he never dies? Drow live centuries. She has decades at most.
