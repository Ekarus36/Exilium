The World of Exilium - Mountains, Distances, and Key Locations

The Major Mountains

The Shieldwall (Central Mountains - Human Side)

Location: Central mountain range, eastern side
Height: Massive peaks, 15,000-20,000 feet
Human Name: "The Shieldwall"

  • Protects Human Kingdom from Elven Empire
  • Nearly impassable except through Veraheim tunnel
  • Rich in gold and iron deposits
  • Human domains cluster on eastern side

Ered Rhain (Central Mountains - Elven Side)

Location: Same range as Shieldwall, western side
Elven Name: "Ered Rhain" (Border Mountains)
Height: Same peaks as Shieldwall

  • Elven name for the central divide
  • Protects Elven Empire from Human Kingdom
  • Veraheim sits at center of this range
  • Elven enclaves on western side

Note: Same mountains, different names based on perspective!

Veraheim's Strategic Position

Why Location Matters

  • Only Safe Passage: Tunnel is the only non-treacherous route through central mountains
  • Equidistant: Equal travel time to both capitals (14 days)
  • Elevation: City sits 500 feet above sea level on plateau
  • Defensible: 500ft cliffs on all sides, three controlled lifts only access
  • Economic Control: All east-west trade must go through Veraheim

Result: Both powers need Veraheim more than they hate it. Neither can afford to lose tunnel access.

The Tunnel

Specifications:

  • Length: ~1 mile through mountain
  • Width: Wide enough for two wagons to pass
  • Height: 15-20 feet (comfortable for mounted riders)
  • Construction: Natural cavern expanded and reinforced by Vera's magic 80 years ago
  • Lighting: Magical lights (powered by Vera) every 50 feet
  • Maintenance: Never needs repair (Vera's magic keeps it stable)

Passage Rules:

  • Toll required for commercial passage
  • Security checks at both ends
  • No weapons drawn in tunnel (instant arrest)
  • Takes full day to process through (lines, inspections)
  • Both powers have equal access

Regional Geography

Western Side (Elven Empire)

  • Ancient forests dominate lowlands
  • 10 Elven Enclaves spread across territory
  • Tirion (capital, 50,000 elves) central location
  • Well-maintained roads between enclaves
  • Climate: Temperate, mild seasons

Eastern Side (Human Kingdom)

  • Rolling plains and farmland
  • 11 Human Domains, nearly autonomous
  • Thornreich (capital) holds strategic center
  • Roads vary (some excellent, some poor)
  • Climate: Temperate, harsher winters than west

The Oceans & Coasts

Coastal Access:

  • Elven Empire: Northwestern coast, several ports
  • Human Kingdom: Southeastern coast, major port cities
  • Veraheim: Harbor at base of southern lift (500ft below city)
  • The Floating Docks: Anchored in open water off Veraheim's harbor

Sea Trade:

  • Possible to sail around the continent (months-long journey)
  • Dangerous waters (storms, pirates, sea monsters)
  • Far more expensive than overland through Veraheim
  • Used only when tunnel access denied or for bulk goods

Campaign Travel

Sample Journeys:

  • Veraheim to nearest town: 2-3 days walk
  • Cross the tunnel: 1 day with processing
  • Veraheim to either capital: 14 days walk, 7 days horse
  • Between Elven Enclaves: 3-7 days typically
  • Between Human Domains: 2-10 days depending on roads

Travel Hazards:

  • Bandits on less-traveled roads
  • Wild animals in forests
  • Mountain passes (avalanches, exposure)
  • Political tensions (Empire vs Kingdom patrols)
  • Monsters (depends on region)

Veraheim's position makes it invaluable - the strategic center that both powers need but neither controls.
